About Us

“E Services” will provide a platform where people can acquire and provide services online. A common platform is provided where service provider and users can interact with each other. Example: Let’s suppose you are a Painter and you are currently free, then you can create an account on “E Services” by registering on our website. After that you have to upload your profile on our website. Now anyone, who needs a painter will open our website, now he can choose a painter from the list of painters depending upon the charges, rating etc. Similarly, they can search any other service like plumber, carpenter, electrician, doctor, motor mechanic, personal gym trainer, tutor, photographer, mason etc.

The main problem faced by service providers is that they face difficulty in finding work. This problem is mostly faced by those service providers who are new in the market. And common people face difficulty in finding service providers. E services can indirectly solve the problem of unemployment by providing a common platform where service provider and users can interact with each other.
